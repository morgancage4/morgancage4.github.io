About Me & This Site:

Hi I'm Morgan Cage and I created this site as a digital diary of all of my travels.
I travel quite often with friends and family and thought it would be a good idea to keep memories of these trips. I have these organized by location as seen by the navigation links and each of these are further divided into either the cities, states, or countries I have visited in each of these areas. Feel free to give me feedback on what I can improve, where and who I should travel with next, and favorite trip and/or memory using the survey link at the bottom of the page. You can also reach out through email by clicking on my email address, which is down below as well. I hope you enjoy, get inspiration for your next travels, or get some reminders of the trips you have shared with me/had on your own!

If you're looking to book your own travels, I recommend looking at Expedia to look for the cheapest flights & hotels, TripAdvisor for reviews and recommendations, and Airbnb for unique stays.
